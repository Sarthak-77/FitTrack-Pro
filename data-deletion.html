Data Deletion Instructions

FitTrack Pro respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your data from our application.

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Log into your FitTrack Pro account
Step 2: Navigate to your Profile page (click your profile icon in the top right)
Step 3: Scroll down to the "Account Settings" section
Step 4: Click on "Delete My Account"
Step 5: Confirm your deletion request
⚠️ Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data including:
  • Profile information
  • Fitness activity logs
  • Meal tracking data
  • Progress history
  • All associated records
This action cannot be undone.

Alternative: Request Data Deletion via Email

If you're unable to access your account or prefer to request deletion via email, please contact us at:

  • Confirmation that you want to delete all your data

Processing Time

Data deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • ✅ Your account information
  • ✅ Personal profile data
  • ✅ All fitness tracking data
  • ✅ Meal and nutrition logs
  • ✅ Activity history
  • ✅ Any photos or media you've uploaded

Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Transaction records (for accounting purposes)
  • Legal compliance requirements
  • Fraud prevention

Such data will be kept only for the minimum required period.
